Welcome!

Welcome to EpiLexO Search, the fruition platform for searching texts and lexicons connected to the EpiLexO editing platform. The platform was created in the context of the PRIN 2017 project: "Languages and cultures of Ancient Italy" and in this context it allows users to consult the texts and lexicons uploaded / created in the editing platform.
